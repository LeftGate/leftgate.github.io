How LeftGate Protects You

LeftGate sits silently on your developers' systems, scanning every commit in real-time before it reaches your version control system. Our advanced pattern recognition detects API keys, passwords, tokens, and other sensitive data instantly.

When a secret is detected, LeftGate immediately blocks the commit and alerts the developer, preventing the exposure before it happens.
